The materials here represent the output from a set of softwares developed for the investigation of the OSKM reprogramming process.

Several outputs utilize 1 dimensional visualization of axies including euclidean interpritations of the data.

These interpritations included the use of distance from the start or end points (skin cells (bjs)) and stem cells (hesc).

The third single dimensional perpective is the progression of a sample along a sraight line 96 dimensional trajectory from the center of the know starter and known destination cells.
